In our enthralling game, players are granted a personalized gaming experience from the get-go,
as they have the freedom to choose their spaceship type, as well as the type of shots that they will wield.
This feature allows for a unique and tailored gameplay experience for each player, making each game session truly one-of-a-kind.

To ensure that players are not left in the dark, we have incorporated comprehensive game instructions,
making it easy for players to familiarize themselves with the game mechanics and start playing with ease and confidence.
As an added bonus, we have included in-game power-ups that offer players a chance to enhance their gameplay.
By collecting the red heart that appears on the game board,
players can add an extra life, effectively prolonging their game time.
Additionally, players can add precious seconds to their game time by reaching the clock that also appears on the game board.
In addition, we use Jquery to validate the sign up process.


With all these exciting features, our game promises to provide players with endless hours of exhilarating gameplay and a truly immersive gaming experience!!!

Spaceships Game

Objective: Kill all the enemies while surviving with at least one life until the timer runs out.

How to Play:
1. Sign up and log in to the game.
2. Use the arrow keys to move your spaceship around the screen.
3. Choose a key to shoot with.
4. Customize your player image, your shots, and the enemy's shots.
5. Set the game timer (minimum is 120 seconds).
6. During the game, collect a heart to gain an additional life, and collect a clock to add 10 seconds to the timer.
7. Shoot and destroy all enemies to win.
8. If the player dies, you lose the game.
9. If the time runs out, the score determines whether you win or lose. If the score is above 100, you win. If it's below 100, you lose.
10. Every 5 seconds, the speed of the enemies and their shots increases, making it more challenging as time passes.
11. After every game, a scores table will appear and show the score, the game status (win or lose), and the timestamp.

Good luck and have fun playing Spaceships Game!
